- Indexing access is not allowed
- All operations are executed with a TC of O(1) **
    - i.e in constant time

PRIORITY QUEUE

- Largest (default) or smallest element stays at the top
- Thus it automatically inserts new elements in between, in order to maintain an asc/desc sequence
- Not a linear data structure
- Inside a tree data structure is maintained
